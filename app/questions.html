Q: What is Cameo?

A: Cameo operates the CAMEO™ Marketplace — through our website and app — that lets fans book a personalized video shout-out — a CAMEO video — from their favorite talent. Our mission is to create the most personalized and authentic fan experiences in the world.

Q: How do I contact the CAMEO™ team?

A: Feel free to contact us here: hello@bookcameo.com. One of the Cameo Fameo will get back to you as soon as possible.

Q: How can I work at Cameo?

A: We’re always looking for rock stars! To view job openings, click on the jobs link on our website.

Q: What else should I know?

A: You can review our Site Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for additional information .

Q: Can I be notified when new talent joins the CAMEO™ Marketplace?

A: Yes! There are 2 ways to be notified when your favorite talent joins Cameo! You can subscribe to our newsletter, or if you request that a certain talent join via the search bar, you can get an email after they do.
